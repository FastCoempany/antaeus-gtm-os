# Triage the week's reads

Opens the Dashboard. The "this week's reads" card surfaces the
workspace-scope observations the heartbeat wrote since your last visit
— stalled deals, silent watched accounts, overdue proofs, quiet
discovery weeks.

The card is the operator's loop:

- Read each observation
- Dismiss the ones that aren't actionable (one click)
- Click into the related room for the ones that are

The 14d / 7d toggle in the card header changes how sensitive the
stalled-deal threshold is. 14d is the default; flip to 7d when you
want to catch earlier-stage decay.
