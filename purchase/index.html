Purchase Path

Make payment the least ambiguous part of the product.

This is the bridge from interest to access. Buy the annual plan, then create or sign into the workspace that will route you through onboarding, the welcome guide, and into the dashboard.

What happens after you pay: you should land on the purchase-success page, create your workspace using the same email from checkout, then move through onboarding and the welcome guide into the durable app.
