## 1. Purpose

This document is the explicit signoff on what Phase 7 is allowed to change and what it is forbidden to weaken inside each module family.

This is not conceptual guidance. It is a preservation gate.

## 2. Global Signoff Rules

Signed off for Phase 7:

- shell hierarchy may change
- room rail behavior may change
- disclosure order may change
- first-fold copy may compress
- methodology may move deeper or become progressively disclosed

Not signed off for Phase 7:

- rewriting GTM frameworks
- weakening room-level rigor
- deleting compounding logic
- flattening strategic seriousness
- genericizing protected named assets

## 4. Protected Named Asset Signoff

Explicit signoff:

- `Signal Console` remains a named premium asset
- `Future Autopsy` remains a named premium asset

Phase 7 may not:
- relabel them into generic utility names
- bury them beneath vague family labels
- reduce their perceived seriousness to make the shell feel lighter

## 5. Protected Product-Thesis Signoff

Explicit signoff:

Antaeus remains:
- a command-first GTM operating system
- object-centric
- behaviorally engineered
- strategically serious
- deeper than generic market SaaS

Phase 7 may not:
- turn it into a room browser with better spacing
- turn it into a dashboard of soft summaries
- weaken the distinction between command, sheet, room, and graph

## 6. Compounding-Logic Signoff

Explicit signoff:

No Phase 7 change may break the compounding logic defined in [phase-46-cross-module-compounding-rules-2026-03-26.md](./phase-46-cross-module-compounding-rules-2026-03-26.md).

That includes:

- ICP driving targeting defaults
- sourcing pushing into Signal Console
- Signal Console heat affecting Dashboard and motion surfaces
- Call Planner handing into Discovery and Deal
- Discovery sharpening Deal and Readiness
- Deal pressure driving Future Autopsy, PoC, Advisor, Dashboard
- Future Autopsy routing into exact corrective rooms
- PoC and Advisor feeding deal truth back upstream
- Quota shaping Dashboard and execution pressure
- Readiness and Handoff remaining system-level truth surfaces

## 7. Signoff Summary

Phase 7 is signed off to proceed only on the condition that:

- the shell changes
- the disclosure changes
- the access model changes

while:

- the module brains do not
- the GTM frameworks do not
- the strategic seriousness does not
- the compounding rules do not

If any planned Phase 7 change violates the tables above, it is out of scope and must be rejected or split into a separate program.
