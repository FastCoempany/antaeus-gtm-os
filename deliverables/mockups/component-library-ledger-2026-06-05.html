The idea

Ledger UI. No cards. The workspace is a ruled record of commercial facts and decisions. Each entry has the same shape — Account · Signal · Move · State — and indents into Evidence, Owner, Due, Candidate beneath it. Inline actions are text links underlined in orange, not button blocks. Section breaks render as ruled headings with serif titles and mono counts.

The native primitives. Ledger row replaces card. Entry group replaces section. Detail band replaces drawer. Inline action replaces CTA block. Evidence line replaces tooltip. The discipline: calm density. Every entry earns its row; every row reads at a glance.

Canon preserved. Slightly warmer paper for the ledger feel. DM Serif Display for account names and group headings. Public Sans for body. JetBrains Mono for column codes, kinds, timestamps. Orange as the rationed accent for action links and active filter.
