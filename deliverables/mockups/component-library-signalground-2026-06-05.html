Signalground

A quiet component grammar for Antaeus.

Three layers, working together. Quiet Command does the base work — a clean modular operating system any VP of Sales would recognize as a working tool. Grounded Signal carries the distinctive flavor through anchored cards, signal spines, decision rails, and a weight hierarchy that says what matters. Modern Inscription lifts the moments that earn weight — the strategic brief, the judgment, the handoff memo. The mood is calm, sturdy, executive, and unmistakably Antaeus without ever looking like a museum or a cockpit.

THREE

The moments. Modern Inscription.

Reserved for surfaces the system has earned: the strategic brief, the autopsy verdict, the handoff memo, the board-ready summary. Used sparingly so it carries weight when it shows up. No drama. Restrained typography, fine inset rules, and a sense that what is written here will be referenced again.
