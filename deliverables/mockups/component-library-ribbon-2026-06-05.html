The idea

Ribbon UI. The recognizable shape language is the thread header — a thin horizontal band with an embedded label trailing off to the right. Sections are tied together visually rather than floating as separate cards. Cards clamp to ribbons through small tabs. State appears as a strip crossing the component. Drawers carry persistent ribbon titles. The active nav item is a full-width ribbon with orange end-caps, not a pill.

The rule. Ribbons organize movement. Cards contain meaning. Buttons trigger action. Keep them separate. Canon preserved throughout — bright field, DM Serif Display reserved for high-importance moments, Public Sans for body, JetBrains Mono for thread labels and codes. Orange as the rationed accent for the dominant move and the active-nav end-caps.
