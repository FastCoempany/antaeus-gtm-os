DESIGN SYSTEM · COMPONENT LIBRARY · CANDIDATES SHEET · 2026-06-05 · DRAFT

What might be in the library

A sheet of candidate primitives the design system's component library could consolidate. Each card shows one primitive at the canon's bright-field direction: navy ink on the cool neutral field, orange as the rationed accent, DM Serif Display for authored emphasis, Public Sans for control surfaces, JetBrains Mono for kickers and meters. This is exploratory — the actual library spec will pick the set, lock the contracts, and specify the migration. For now: what the candidates look like, grouped by role.
