The idea

Lens UI. A second geometry — soft asymmetric ovals with quiet aperture rings — introduced inside an otherwise rectangular SaaS workspace. The lens shows up only for what the system has actually evaluated: next-best-action, risk explanation, forecast judgment, account summary, AI recommendation. The CTA attaches to the lens, not the card edge. Tooltips render as small circular callouts. Selected rows expand into lens previews inline.

Canon preserved. Bright field. DM Serif Display for lens signal copy and headlines. Public Sans for body. JetBrains Mono for codes and timestamps. Orange for the dominant move and the lens aperture ring; canon color roles intact. The discipline: never put a chip in a bubble. Bubble plague is real. The lens is reserved for the moments the system has earned.
