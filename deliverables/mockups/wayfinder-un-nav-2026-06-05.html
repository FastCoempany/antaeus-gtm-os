The rules
this is testing

  • The hallway is gone. No left rail. The Wayfinder replaces both nav and triage with one ranked read of where the work is, always visible, never insistent. The 22 rooms exist; they're summoned through Ctrl+K, not browsed.
  • Agency stays with the operator. The bar shows a read, never opens a room without an explicit commit. The resting workspace is genuinely neutral. Click-deeper is an explicit gesture — hover does nothing.
  • Verbs first. Search falls back. Ctrl+K is "what's next?" not "where do I go?" Top section is the system's contextual picks. Below that, verbs the operator can type to act on anything. Below that, rooms to go to.
  • The bar persists across rooms. Birdseye Float gets promoted from a corner widget to the primary wayfinding element. There is no corner icon. The bar at the top is doing that job, more legibly.
  • Voice rule. No compound nouns the product invented to feel important. "Pilot results," not "cast proof." "Two-line note," not "outbound motion." Written so it sounds like the operator next to you talking.
