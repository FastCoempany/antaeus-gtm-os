What this is testing

  • Ranks but never imposes. You land on a calm brief, not your whole pipeline ranked and waiting. The ranked list (Queue) is one switch away, opt-in, never the arrival.
  • The Brief is the body, and it's actionable. Pressure read → what changed → the one move (takeable here) → at most one real insight. Not read-only, not a wall of text.
  • Never "all done." The quiet day names what's compounding and turns the loop forward. The home never goes blank on a good day.
  • It stays in its lane. The week's reads is a glance; the full check-in is the Briefing room. The home routes to rooms; it isn't where the work is done; it isn't a feed.
  • Honest at the edges. New, sparse, and can't-see states are real and directional — never a broken or empty dashboard, never a confident wrong ranking.
  • Canon held. Bright field; DM Serif Display for the read, Public Sans for the work, JetBrains Mono for codes; orange on the one move only; every sentence a plain peer's voice.
