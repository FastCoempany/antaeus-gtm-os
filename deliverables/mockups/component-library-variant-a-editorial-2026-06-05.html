The Antaeus Sheet

Candidate primitives, rendered as a long-form journal.

The editorial cut treats every primitive as if it were a column in a research journal. Serif type carries the narrative weight, ruled borders structure the field, italics carry softer states, mono recedes to footnotes and timestamps. The bones are the same; the voice of the surfaces shifts toward considered authorship.
