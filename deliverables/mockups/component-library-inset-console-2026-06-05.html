Inset Console.

Important work feels set into the workspace, not slapped on top.

Most SaaS products lift every card with shadows. Antaeus goes the other direction: content panels are recessed wells, inputs sink into the surface, the active nav item depresses into the rail. Buttons stay raised because they are the things that act. The surface reads as worked into, not floating above. The silhouette is physical without being skeuomorphic — shallow, matte, and quiet.

The idea

Inset Console. Content lives in shallow wells with inner shadow and a faint highlight on the bottom edge. Inputs feel pressed into the form. The active nav item depresses into the rail. Tables are rows in shallow grooves. AI recommendations render inside tinted answer wells — set into the workspace, never floating above. Buttons stay raised because they are the things that act; raised against the recessed surface gives them obvious affordance.

Canon preserved. Bright field (slightly cooler tint for the recessed look). DM Serif Display for headlines and stats. Public Sans for body and controls. JetBrains Mono for codes, IDs, and the Signal / Reason / Move labels. Orange as the rationed accent for the dominant move and the answer well. Shallow, matte, spacious — too much inset reads old-school; the discipline is restraint.
