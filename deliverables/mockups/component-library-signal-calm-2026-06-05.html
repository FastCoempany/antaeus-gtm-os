Signal Calm.

Everything is calm until something truly matters.

A restrained SaaS surface built around quiet signal hierarchy. Most of the product is plain. Cards are unmarked until a risk or action appears. Alerts are a thin side glow, never a giant warning block. The repeated structural unit — Signal / Reason / Move — becomes a product-native grammar the operator and the inheritor both learn to read.

The idea

Signal Calm. Most of the product is plain. The signature is the repeated Signal / Reason / Move grammar — the operator and the inheritor both learn to read it once and recognize it everywhere. Risk and attention show up as restrained side-glow marks, never as cockpit chrome.

Canon preserved. Bright field. DM Serif Display reserved for headlines and stats. Public Sans dominant. JetBrains Mono only for codes, IDs, and the small uppercase labels that carry the Signal/Reason/Move structure. Orange for the dominant move; all canon color roles intact.
