# Antaeus Discovery Studio Lumana On-Call Control Lock

Status: hard control lock  
Date: 2026-04-10

Purpose: convert the Lumana Discovery reference HTML files into explicit Antaeus Discovery Studio operating constraints.

## Source artifacts now mirrored into the strict bible

- `07-control-artifacts/lumana-discovery-command-center-reference-2026-04-10.html`
- `07-control-artifacts/lumana-discovery-framework-reference-2026-04-10.html`

These are not palette references.
They are operating references.

Antaeus does not need to copy Lumana's category, visual palette, or exact layout.
It must match or exceed the live-call discipline those files enforce.

## What the references prove

1. Discovery is an operating console, not a framework gallery.
2. Time pressure must be visible, felt, and actionable while the call is live.
3. Compression cannot be abstract. It needs an explicit essentials mode.
4. Skip-ahead recovery is first-class behavior, not an appendix.
5. Value deployment must be staged. Some truth is held, some truth is deployed now.
6. Support intelligence must remain reachable without becoming the main canvas.
7. The user needs exact speakable language plus branching, not teaching prose.
8. Resumption state must be obvious after interruptions, objections, or time cuts.
9. Post-call structure matters, but it cannot outrank live-call control.

## Hard constraints now adopted for Discovery Studio

- A visible call clock with urgency state is mandatory.
- Phase tempo guidance is mandatory.
  - The room must show when the rep is on pace, tight, or over pace.
  - The room must expose explicit jump guidance when the rep is over pace.
- Compression mode is mandatory.
  - Compression mode must reduce the room to essential nodes, not merely recolor the page.
- Skip-ahead handlers are mandatory.
  - Demo request, pricing question, send-info brush-off, wrong-person redirect, and time-pressure recovery must be reachable without leaving the main room.
- A support dossier is mandatory.
  - The rep must have category-native proof, deployment anchors, security or implementation facts, and key account intelligence within one move.
- Tie-back discipline is mandatory.
  - Discovery Studio must distinguish between truth to hold and truth to deploy now.
- Objection and inbound-question handling are mandatory from the live surface.
- Phase progress and resumption state are mandatory.
- Learned-fact capture must sit close to live node work, not in a separate note graveyard.

## Translation into Antaeus Discovery Studio

This is how Lumana control behavior maps into the Antaeus room:

- `framework worksheet rail`
  - Replaces generic category browsing.
  - Lets the rep lock the correct category machine fast.
- `lead-sheet switcher`
  - Becomes the active node engine.
  - The rep should be able to advance the conversation by switching the active sheet and its response branches.
- `response rail`
  - Must expose real buyer reactions, not decorative alternatives.
- `call clock + tempo state`
  - Must sit above or adjacent to the live surface.
  - The rep should know whether they are on pace or need to jump.
- `compression toggle`
  - Must collapse the room to essential live-call moves.
- `skip-ahead tray`
  - Must recover the call when the buyer jumps to demo, pricing, competitor, send-info, or wrong-person motions.
- `support dossier`
  - Must keep product-category proof, proof threshold clues, deployment reality, and objection anchors reachable.
- `tieback ledger`
  - Must store the truths that have been earned and whether they should be held or deployed now.
- `next-step lock`
  - Must remain visible as the room's end condition.
- `handoff routes`
  - Must preserve discovery truth into Deal Workspace, Call Planner, and Future Autopsy without restatement.

## What we do not copy literally

- Lumana's security-specific copy
- Lumana's visual palette
- Lumana's exact three-column layout
- Dense support chrome that competes with the live node
- Any overlap, stacking, or ornamental motion that makes live use harder

## Non-negotiable build tests now derived from these references

Discovery Studio fails if any of the following are not true:

- The rep can recover from a buyer interruption in one move.
- The rep can switch into compression mode without losing the next-step path.
- The rep can answer a buyer question without abandoning discovery control.
- The rep can see what to say, what to listen for, and where to go next without reading paragraphs.
- The rep can resume after being interrupted, challenged, or delayed.
- The room preserves a distinction between earned facts, support proof, and deploy-now language.

## Design warning

The Lumana files are strong because they are strict about live-call behavior, not because they are visually maximal.

If Antaeus copies their density without preserving their operating discipline, the result will be worse than both.
The inheritance we want is control logic, not clutter.
