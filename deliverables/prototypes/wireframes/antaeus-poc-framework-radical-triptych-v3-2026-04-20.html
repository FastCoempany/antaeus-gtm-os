PoC Framework / Radical Round Three
Antaeus

Proof is not a page. It is a forced event.

This round leaves normal product grammar behind. The PoC room either behaves like a launch gate, a proof hearing, or a foundry that turns raw belief into evidence the buyer's boss can act on.

Variant 01 / Launch Gantry

A pilot should not launch casually.

What it means: the room is a pre-launch gantry. The operator clears interlocks before a PoC can leave the pad. This makes “run pilot” feel consequential, not like clicking another button.

Hold launch until the evidence path is sealed.

Variant 03 / Proof Foundry

Melt vague belief into a usable artifact.

What it means: the room is an industrial proof forge. Claims enter as raw ore. The system casts them through baseline, owner, metric, and kill-rule molds. The output is a proof ingot that can be carried into Deal Workspace or Advisor Deploy.

Raw interest is not proof until it can be carried.
