Variant 01 Descendants · Zero Box Study

Three new variants built from the strongest prior direction, but with the dead objects removed: no timer, no ornamental chips, no card shells, no boxiness. `Buyer might say` and `Say next` here are visual stand-ins for the real fork engine, where those lines would keep changing as the conversation advances.
