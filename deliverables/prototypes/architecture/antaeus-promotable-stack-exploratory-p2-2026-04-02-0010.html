Exploratory Surface P2

Rendered into the light.

This is the user’s promotable-stack idea run in-browser as a side experiment. It is not Grid, not Queue, and not a new baseline.

Why this exists

A fourth surface, if it ever existed.

The stack on the right is deliberately simpler than the spotlight. Clicking a row promotes it into the light. That is why this cannot quietly masquerade as Grid.

What is preserved

State badge, signal heat or quality metric, and gap count all stay visible in the stack.

What this proves

If you ever wanted a promotable surface, this is the honest way to do it: make the promotion behavior explicit instead of hiding it inside Grid semantics.

Exploratory promotion model

One spotlight. One promotable stack.

The right column is now intentionally leaner. Click any row and it becomes the spotlight object on the left.
