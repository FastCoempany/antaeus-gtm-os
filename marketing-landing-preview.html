Questions

Who is this actually for?
The primary buyer is the founder or first GTM operator at a founder-led B2B company who knows the motion is still trapped in people's heads. It also works just after the first senior AE or founding seller joins, when you need the system they should have inherited on day one.
Is this a CRM?
No. It's the intelligence and methodology layer that sits above your CRM. It captures what the CRM can't: your sales knowledge, your process, your loss patterns, your discovery frameworks. It tells you what to do next. Your CRM records that you did it.
Should I buy before or after hiring the first AE?
Preferably before, because the whole point is to build the handoff kit and operating system before you make an expensive hire. But if you already hired them, use Antaeus to stop forcing them to reverse-engineer the motion from scraps.
Is there a free trial or demo?
There is a demo workspace. Use that first if you want to see the system think with seeded data. The paid plan is annual because this is operating infrastructure, not a toy you poke for three days and forget.
What happens after I pay?
You land on the purchase-success page, then create an account or sign in with the same email from checkout. From there the app routes you through onboarding, the welcome guide, and into the real workspace. In the first session you should be able to create your first ICP and start feeding real GTM truth into the system.
Can my whole team use it?
One purchase is currently one user. The cleanest use case is the founder or first GTM operator building the motion. If you need multi-seat or advisor/client usage, email hello@antaeus.app and handle it intentionally instead of pretending this is generic team software.
