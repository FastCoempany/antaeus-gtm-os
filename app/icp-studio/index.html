Scoring Guide
● 70-100 — Strong: Tier 1 — prioritize and sequence first
● 40-69 — Moderate: Tier 2 — work if capacity allows
● 0-39 — Weak: Tier 3 — park or disqualify
Examples: 73 — Strong, 52 — Moderate, 31 — Weak. This score reflects weighted criteria. Two ICPs within 5 points are effectively equivalent.

Diagnostics
Quick checks to avoid common mistakes.
Thin ICP is right when:
  • You can name 50 accounts that match
  • You can find a trigger in <10 minutes
  • Your statement fits in one sentence
  • A rep can repeat it without reading
Thin ICP is wrong when:
  • Your statement includes 3+ industries
  • Your buyer is "anyone"
  • Your use case list is longer than your pipeline
  • Your trigger is "they should care"
