PoC Framework

No clear success criteria, no pilot. Scope tightly and force a decision.

What this produces
  • Scoped hypothesis to test
  • Success criteria defined upfront
  • Boundaries that prevent scope creep
The rules
  • 7-14 days max - no endless trials
  • Written success criteria before kickoff
  • Executive sponsor at readout
What breaks PoCs
  • No hypothesis - just "try it"
  • Success criteria written after
  • No decision maker at readout
