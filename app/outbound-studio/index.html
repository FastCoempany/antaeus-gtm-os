The CTA Escalation Ladder

The ladder only moves up. Never skip a rung. Match the ask to the trust level.

Give-to-Get-to-Give Loop
GIVE (value with no ask) → GET (small commitment) → GIVE (over-deliver) → GET (bigger commitment) → repeat.

Ice Cold: Give insight → Get a reply
Cool: Give one-pager/framework → Get 15 min call
Warm: Give account-specific analysis → Get 30 min meeting
Hot: Give business case → Get champion commitment
Closing: Give implementation plan → Get signature

The test: "Would I still send this if they never buy from me?" If yes, it's a real give. If no, it's a pitch.
Three Laws of Outbound
1. Every touch must earn the next one. Before pressing send: "Does this give the recipient a reason to want to hear from me again?"

2. The CTA must match the temperature. A cold prospect should never get "book a demo." A warm prospect should never get "just checking in."

3. Give before you ask. Then give again. Lead with value. Earn a commitment. Deliver more than expected. Repeat.
