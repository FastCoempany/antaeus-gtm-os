Understand Your Tiers
Tiers define how much of your selling time an account receives. This isn't a ranking — it's a resource allocation commitment. Choose carefully.
TierInvestmentCapacityWhat It Means
Tier 1 Maximum intensity 30–50 accounts Multi-channel, multi-threaded. Custom angles. Highest conviction bets.
Tier 2 Structured cadence 75–100 accounts Consistent but lighter. Room for escalation when signals fire.
Tier 3 Signal-triggered Remainder (up to 300) Efficient, systematic. Watch for triggers that warrant escalation.
