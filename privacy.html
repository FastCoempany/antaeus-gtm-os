Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Antaeus collects, uses, discloses, and stores information when you visit the site, create an account, use the product, or interact with related services.

LAST UPDATED: MARCH 22, 2026

1. Who this Policy applies to

This Policy applies to Antaeus and the Antaeus website and product available at antaeus.app. Questions about this Policy can be sent to hello@antaeus.app.

2. What information we collect

Depending on how you use Antaeus, we may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information, such as your name, email address, role, account identifiers, and authentication metadata.
  • Workspace information, such as the inputs, notes, scores, plans, exports, call prep, deal records, and operating data you enter or generate in the product.
  • Usage information, such as page views, product interactions, referral information, UTM attribution, browser type, device type, and basic technical diagnostics.
  • Browser-stored information, such as local storage, session storage, and similar browser-side state used for preferences, draft state, demo mode, tour state, and other product continuity.
  • Commercial information, such as plan or checkout status and limited transaction metadata, when paid access is offered through a third-party payment provider.
  • Communications, such as emails you send to us and authentication emails sent by the service.

3. How we collect information

  • Directly from you when you create an account, complete onboarding, enter workspace data, request a password reset, or contact us.
  • Automatically when you browse or use the service, including through analytics, logs, local storage, session storage, and similar technologies.
  • From service providers that support authentication, hosting, email delivery, analytics, and payment processing.

4. How we use information

We use information to operate, secure, and improve Antaeus, including to:

  • create and manage accounts and authenticate users;
  • persist workspace data and product progress;
  • power product features, exports, reminders, and guidance;
  • analyze traffic, funnel performance, and product usage;
  • send transactional emails such as signup confirmation and password reset messages;
  • support customers and respond to inquiries;
  • detect abuse, fraud, bugs, and security incidents; and
  • comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.

5. How Antaeus stores data today

Antaeus currently uses a mixed storage model.

  • Cloud-backed data: core account, authentication, and major durable workspace state may be stored through our Supabase-backed persistence layer.
  • Browser-stored data: some UI preferences, draft state, attribution state, demo mode state, welcome state, tour state, and similar values may still be stored in your browser through local storage or session storage.
  • Exported data: if you use the backup/export features, a copy of workspace data may be written to a file you download and store yourself.

This means some data should survive refresh, re-login, and device changes, while some browser-scoped values may not. Clearing storage, switching browsers, or using private browsing can remove locally stored values.

6. Demo mode and seeded data

Antaeus includes demo and seed flows that can write sample workspace data into the browser so you can explore the app with populated modules. Demo data is intended for product exploration and may be stored differently from real account data. If you do not want sample data in your browser, do not launch the seeded demo flow.

7. Analytics and attribution

We use analytics and attribution tooling to understand how people find and use Antaeus. That may include first-touch and last-touch UTM parameters, page and event analytics, and basic device or browser information.

At the time of this update, Antaeus supports Google Analytics 4 and may use additional analytics tooling when configured. We use that data to measure traffic, product engagement, and commercial performance.

8. Email delivery and authentication messages

Authentication emails such as signup confirmations and password reset messages are delivered through Supabase Auth using our configured email delivery provider. At the time of this update, Antaeus uses Resend for outbound auth email delivery from hello@antaeus.app.

9. Payments and checkout

If paid access is offered, checkout is handled by a third-party payment provider. We may receive limited transaction metadata such as checkout status, plan information, and billing-related identifiers. We do not store full payment card details on our own systems.

10. Service providers and disclosures

We may share information with service providers that help us operate the service, including providers for hosting, authentication, email delivery, analytics, and payment processing. At the time of this update, those may include Cloudflare, Supabase, Resend, analytics providers, and a payment processor when checkout is enabled.

We may also disclose information if required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, security, or property of Antaeus, our users, or others.

11. Data retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the service, maintain security, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and support legitimate business operations. Retention periods vary by data type and whether the data is stored in our systems, a provider's systems, or only in your browser.

12. Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

13. Your choices and rights

You can choose whether to create an account, whether to use demo mode, and whether to export local backups. You can also contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of account-linked information we control, subject to verification and applicable law.

If you want to make a privacy-related request, email hello@antaeus.app.

14. Third-party links and services

The site and product may link to or interact with third-party services. Their own terms and privacy policies apply to those services, and we are not responsible for their privacy practices.

15. Children's privacy

Antaeus is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

16. Changes to this Policy

If we update this Policy, we will post the revised version here and update the "Last Updated" date. If required by law, we will provide additional notice for material changes.

17. Contact

Questions or requests about this Privacy Policy can be sent to hello@antaeus.app.
