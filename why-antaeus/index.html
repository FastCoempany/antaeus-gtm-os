The operating system the founder's motion becomes before a hire takes it over.

You already have a CRM. You don't need another sales tool, another AI copilot, or another dashboard. WHY THIS EXISTS

Most founder-led revenue dies in the handoff, not the motion.

The founder runs the first $2M from memory. The motion works because they've been on every call, written every follow-up, decided every concession. Then they sign a hire — and the hire inherits no system, no ranked next move, no record of what made the founder right. By month three, the founder is back to running the motion themselves, and the new hire is being "ramped" for the second time.

The pattern is so common it has a category: founder-to-first-operator handoff failure. CRMs don't fix it because CRMs store what gets typed in, not how the founder thinks. AI copilots don't fix it because the copilot doesn't know which deals are real. Dashboards don't fix it because they describe the motion, they don't run it.

Antaeus is built around one decision: the founder's motion should be a system the hire can walk into. Not export. Not a handoff doc. The workspace itself, sharpened by what the founder did this quarter, ready to inherit.

HOW IT WORKS

A four-step loop the founder runs, the hire inherits.

Every move on this loop compounds. Signal becomes motion, motion becomes a deal, a deal produces evidence, and that evidence becomes the kit the next operator inherits. The loop is the product; the rooms are just where each step is shaped.

STEP 01

Signal

A time-limited event implying a commercial opportunity. Ranked by heat, source, recency, and ICP match — surfaced as ranked accounts to act on, not a passive timeline.

STEP 02

Motion

A specific outbound move with route and intent. Composed in Outbound Studio, Cold Call Studio, or LinkedIn Playbook — never templated spam, always account × buyer × temperature × trigger.

STEP 03

Deal + Evidence

An opportunity tracked by stage, value, and pressure — diagnosed in Deal Workspace, pre-mortemed in Future Autopsy, anchored by pilot evidence the buyer's boss can act on when stakes get serious.

STEP 04

Handoff

Seven authored sections the first hire opens on day one: who hits and who misses, the rails that worked, the questions that earned the next meeting, where deals are won and where they leak.
