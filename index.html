No. $299 buys a full year of access. The App Academy walks you from Readiness Score 0 to 80+ with step-by-step instructions. If the 70% failure rate doesn't convince you this matters, a free trial won't either.

No. Give them the app. The Academy works for new hires too — the Readiness Score reframes from "are you ready to hire" to "how systematized is your GTM motion." It's the playbook they wish existed on day one.
